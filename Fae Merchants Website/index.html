I wanted to become a superhero, but... here I am, a guild.

As a 50-player guild, we do not necessarily want to become a "top-of-the-line", best-of-the-best" guild, that is not our job, and not our decision. We want to allow users to indulge in the story that we carefully craft around the world that we are guests in. Storytellers, players, and worldbuilders are welcome to join, and tell their stories with our own.

  • Master Trades and Crafts
  • Neutral to All Guilds
  • Story-driven Roleplay
  • Endgame Sea-Content
  • Dungeons
  • Defensive Wars
  • Guild Social Events(Dress Code)
  • Caravan on Land and Sea
  • World Bosses

To boldly go...

As you arrive into Fae, you will have three paths ahead of you. Will you become a diplomatic strategist and join into the commands division? Maybe focus on trades and crafts at the science division? Or, perhaps, join the offensive as a mercenary in the operations division?

Command

  • Lead Battles
  • Participate in Alliance and War Meetings
  • Provide Council
  • Diplomacy and Negotiations

Science

  • Master Merchants
  • Crafts and Trades
  • Husbandry
  • Craft Material Loot Priority

Operation

  • Security
  • Mercenary
  • Focus in PVP and PVE Content
  • Equipment Loot Priority

Shh, let's keep this between us!

We are pretty secretive about our requirements to get into and stay within the guild, but if you would like to know than feel free to get a taste of our requirements to join.

We expect our potential members to:

  • Share Player Logs(Including Combat Logs)
  • Must have a Respectable background within the server(s) that they Join.
  • Honor Discussions, not the Argument
  • Maintain Respectable Time within the Chapter they are Wishing to Join.

There may be a delay before conducting your interview, as we are processing numerous applications.

Feel free to introduce yourself in the discord server and get to know everyone.
